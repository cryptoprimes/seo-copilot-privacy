Introduction

SEO Copilot ("we", "our", "the Service") is a Telegram bot that helps website owners analyze and improve their search engine rankings using data from Google Search Console.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Service.

Information We Collect

Google Search Console Data

When you connect your Google account, we access the following data in read-only mode:

  • Search queries and keywords
  • Page URLs
  • Clicks, impressions, and click-through rates
  • Average search positions
  • Country and device statistics

Account Information

  • Telegram user ID and username
  • Subscription status and payment history (Telegram Stars transactions)
We Do NOT Collect:
  • Your Google account password
  • Personal emails or contacts
  • Any data outside of Google Search Console
  • Browsing history or personal files

How We Use Your Data

  • Analytics: Display your SEO performance metrics and trends
  • Opportunities: Identify ranking improvement opportunities
  • Alerts: Notify you about significant changes in your rankings
  • AI Recommendations: Generate personalized SEO suggestions (Pro feature)
  • Reports: Create daily digests and weekly reports

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in encrypted databases on Cloudflare infrastructure
  • OAuth refresh tokens are encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption
  • We use read-only access to Google Search Console â€” we cannot modify your data
  • Data is automatically deleted when you disconnect a site

Data Retention

  • Search Console data is retained while your site is connected
  • When you disconnect a site, all associated data is permanently deleted
  • Account data is retained until you request deletion

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we store about you via the bot
  • Export: Download your data in CSV format (Pro feature)
  • Delete: Disconnect your site to delete all associated data
  • Revoke Access: Remove our access via Google Account Permissions

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Search Console API: To fetch your website analytics
  • Telegram Bot API: To deliver the service
  • Google Gemini AI: To generate SEO recommendations (Pro feature)
  • Cloudflare: For hosting and data storage

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the Telegram bot.
